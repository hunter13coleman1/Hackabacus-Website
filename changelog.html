Changelog

What's new in Hackabacus

Every update, improvement, and fix shipped to the Hackabacus Excel and PowerPoint plugin. Follow along as we build the best finance toolkit available.

v2.4.0 March 2026

AI-Assisted Formula Review & Batch Slides

  • Introduced AI-assisted formula review in beta — scans models for broken references, circular logic, and hardcoded assumptions before they reach your MD's desk
  • Batch slide generation now lets you produce entire pitch decks from a single data source, mapping Excel tables directly to PowerPoint templates
  • Rebuilt the number formatting engine for faster parsing, better edge-case handling, and support for additional GAAP and IFRS conventions
v2.3.1 February 2026

Stability & Compatibility Fixes

  • Resolved intermittent COM add-in stability issues that caused the plugin to disconnect during long Excel sessions
  • Improved compatibility with Excel 365 semi-annual and current channel builds, including the latest ribbon API changes
  • Fixed a rare crash when applying formatting presets to merged cell ranges
v2.3.0 January 2026

Custom Presets & Team Collaboration

  • Custom formatting presets let you save and share your firm's exact formatting rules as reusable one-click configurations
  • Team collaboration sync keeps styles, templates, and presets in lockstep across your entire team with admin-controlled brand consistency
  • Added new PowerPoint templates including waterfall charts, IC memo layouts, and deal overview slides purpose-built for PE workflows
v2.2.0 November 2025

Model Audit Trail & Performance Overhaul

  • Model audit trail tracks every change to your financial model with timestamped logs — see who modified which cells, when, and what the previous values were
  • Complete rewrite of the cell styling engine for more reliable formatting across complex workbooks with heavy conditional formatting
  • 40% performance improvement across all formatting operations through optimized COM interop calls and smarter batch processing
v2.1.0 September 2025

PowerPoint Template Library & Chart Tools

  • Launched the PowerPoint template library with 40+ finance-ready slide layouts including deal overview, org structure, and waterfall chart templates
  • New chart formatting tools automatically apply institutional styling to PowerPoint charts, matching your firm's color palette and typography
  • Multi-monitor support ensures the Hackabacus task pane and dialogs render correctly across mixed-DPI display configurations
v2.0.0 July 2025

Hackabacus 2.0 — Complete Redesign

  • Complete UI overhaul with a redesigned ribbon, modernized task pane, and streamlined workflows that cut clicks in half across every major feature
  • One-click number formatting applies institutional-grade formats to any Excel range instantly — supports GAAP, IFRS, and PE-standard conventions
