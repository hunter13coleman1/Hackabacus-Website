What we're building next

A transparent look at where Hackabacus is headed. See what we've shipped, what's in progress, and what's on deck for finance teams everywhere.

Completed

4 features

One-click number formatting

Shipped

Apply institutional-grade number formats to any Excel range in a single click. Supports GAAP, IFRS, and PE-standard conventions including parenthetical negatives and basis-point notation.

Excel cell styling engine

Shipped

Enforce firm-wide formatting standards across workbooks. Automatically styles headers, inputs, outputs, and check cells to match your team's style guide.

PowerPoint template library

Shipped

Curated library of 40+ finance-ready slide layouts. Includes deal overview, waterfall charts, org structures, and IC memo templates built for banking and PE workflows.

Model audit trail

Shipped

Track every change to your financial model with timestamped logs. See who modified which cells, when, and what the previous values were for full audit compliance.
