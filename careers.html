Why Hackabacus

What it's like to work here

Remote-first

Work from anywhere in the world. We believe great work happens when you have the freedom to design your own environment. No mandatory office days, no relocation required.

Competitive equity

Every team member receives meaningful equity ownership. You're not just building a product; you're building something you own a real piece of. Competitive salary and benefits, too.

Build for finance pros

Our users are some of the sharpest, most demanding professionals in the world. You'll solve hard problems with tight performance constraints and get direct feedback from people who care deeply about their tools.

Small team, big impact

No layers of bureaucracy. Every person on the team has outsized influence on the product, the culture, and the direction of the company. Ship features that reach thousands of finance professionals in days, not quarters.
