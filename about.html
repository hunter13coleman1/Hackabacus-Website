Hackabacus is on a mission to eliminate the tedious, repetitive work that consumes the best hours of analysts, associates, and directors at the world's top firms. We build tools that let you focus on what actually matters: the analysis.

Our Mission

Reclaiming lost hours for the finance industry

Every day, thousands of analysts and associates at private equity firms, investment banks, and infrastructure funds spend hours on repetitive formatting tasks. Aligning decimals. Standardizing fonts. Reformatting pitch decks. Adjusting number formats across hundreds of cells. It's work that has to be done, but it shouldn't take this long.

Hackabacus was founded to solve that problem. Our Excel and PowerPoint plugin suite automates the formatting workflows that eat into your day, giving you back the time to focus on financial modeling, deal analysis, and the strategic thinking that drives real value.

What We Believe

The principles behind everything we build

Speed

Every millisecond matters when you're closing a deal. Our tools are engineered for instant response so you never lose momentum during a live model or last-minute pitch revision.

Precision

Institutional-grade formatting that meets the highest standards. From GAAP-compliant number formats to pixel-perfect slide layouts, every detail is handled with care.

Reliability

Tools that work every time, because your deadlines don't wait. We obsess over stability and testing so you can trust Hackabacus at 2 AM before a partner review.

Simplicity

Powerful features that feel intuitive from day one. No steep learning curves, no bloated menus. Just clean, purposeful design that gets out of your way.

The Problem We Solve

Finance teams deserve better tools

Studies consistently show that PE analysts, investment bankers, and finance professionals spend 30% or more of their working hours on formatting and presentation tasks rather than substantive analysis. That's hundreds of hours per year per person spent on Excel formatting, PowerPoint automation, and slide standardization instead of financial modeling, valuation analysis, and deal execution.

The existing tools on the market were built in a different era. They're slow, bloated, and designed without genuine input from the people who use them daily. Hackabacus takes a different approach. We work directly with deal teams at leading firms to understand their actual workflows, then build focused, high-performance tools that address real pain points.

Whether you're formatting a three-statement model, standardizing a 200-page CIM, or preparing a management presentation deck under a tight deadline, Hackabacus gives you the speed and precision to deliver institutional-quality work in a fraction of the time.
